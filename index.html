Welcome!

My name is Caroline Whitaker, I am a licensed clinical mental health counselor practicing in Knoxville, Tennessee. I specialize in eating disorders, mood disorders and anxiety, grief, adjustments, OCD, ADHD, stress management, self-esteem and body image issues.

Specialties

In addition to general mental health counseling, I specialize in the following areas:

Eating Disorders

Anorexia Nervosa, Bulimia Nervosa, Binge Eating Disorder, ARFID, OSFED

Mood Disorders

Major Depressive Disorder, Persistent Depressive Disorder, Bipolar I/II

Anxiety Disorders

Generalized Anxiety Disorder, Panic Disorder, Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder

Adjustment Disorders

Major life transitions, grief/loss, identity development, relationship difficulties, etc.

ADHD

Inattentive, Hyperactive/Impulsive, and Combined types

Self-Esteem and Body Image

You don't have to have an eating disorder to struggle with self-worth!
